Dr. Saumya Nayak

Dr. Saumya Nayak is a renowned Plastic, Cosmetic, Hair Transplant, and Microvascular Surgeon based in Vadodara, Gujarat. He holds a DNB in Plastic Surgery from Ganga Hospital, Coimbatore, and a Fellowship in Advanced Cosmetic Surgery (FACS) from Mumbai.

He underwent specialized training in Cosmetic Surgery at the prestigious 'Manhattan Eye, Ear, and Throat Hospital' and the 'New York Eye and Ear Infirmary of Mount Sinai,' USA. Additionally, he completed the APSI IXth IPRAS National Travelling Fellowship in Cosmetic Surgery at Allure MedSpa, Mumbai, and a Hair Transplant Fellowship at Medispa, Jaipur.

Dr. Nayak has also earned the APSI KEM Microsurgery Fellowship at Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai, and PGIEMR Chandigarh. He further honed his skills at the Charles Pinto Centre in Kerala, focusing on Cleft Lip, Palate, Maxillofacial, and Orthognathic surgeries.

With publications in esteemed journals like the Indian Journal of Plastic Surgery (IJPS) and the Journal of Plastic Reconstructive and Aesthetic Surgery (JPRAS), Dr. Nayak's expertise reflects his dedication to advancing his field.

Dr. Suhani Nayak

Dr. Suhani Nayak is a distinguished ophthalmologist based in Vadodara, Gujarat. She earned her M.B.B.S. from Smt. N.H.L Municipal Medical College, Ahmedabad, Gujarat University, followed by an M.S. in Ophthalmology from Shri C.H. Nagri Eye Hospital, Ahmedabad, Gujarat University.

Dr. Suhani has served as a Consultant Ophthalmologist (Cataract & Medical Retina) at Lotus Eye Hospital and Institute, Coimbatore, and as Senior Registrar (SR) at GMERS Medical College and Hospital, Gotri, and SSG Hospital, Baroda. She is currently affiliated with Parul Sevashram Hospital, Baroda.

She has completed a specialized Fellowship in "Retinopathy of Prematurity and Pediatric Retinal Disorders" and "Lasers for Diabetic Retinopathy Medical Retina" at Aravind Eye Hospital, Coimbatore. Additionally, she was awarded the prestigious ICO Fellowship in "Ocular Inflammation & Uveitis" at The New York Eye and Ear Infirmary of Mount Sinai, USA, sponsored by the International Council of Ophthalmology (ICO). She has also undertaken an observership in Uveitis at PGIMER, Chandigarh, under Dr. Amod Gupta.

Dr. Suhanis research credentials include third place in the All India Young Research Awards session at AIOC 2015. Her work on "Comparison of Macular Photocoagulation Alone or Combined with Intravitreal Anti-VEGF (Bevacizumab) for Diabetic Macular Edema" has been widely recognized. She has contributed to over 20 research papers and posters, solidifying her reputation as an accomplished academic and clinician.
