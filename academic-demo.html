Academics & Research

DNB (Plastic Surgery), Board-Certified Plastic, Cosmetic & Hair Transplant Surgeon

Dr. Saumyakumar (Saumya) Nayak

Dr. Saumyakumar Nayak is a board-certified Plastic, Cosmetic & Hair Transplant Surgeon, Director & Founder of Luxor Hospital, Vadodara. His academic work focuses on high-definition body contouring, advanced laser applications in aesthetic surgery and evidence-based cosmetic outcomes.

    VASER body contouring, CO2 lasers in aesthetic & reconstructive surgery, gynecomastia, facial plastic surgery & hair restoration.

Research & Affiliations

Areas of Interest & Memberships

Dr. Nayak’s clinical research is centred around safe and reproducible aesthetic outcomes using modern technologies like VASER-assisted body contouring and CO2 lasers, along with minimally invasive approaches for gynecomastia, facial rejuvenation and hair restoration.

Primary Research Interests

  • • VASER body contouring & high-definition liposuction
  • • CO2 lasers in aesthetic & reconstructive surgery
  • • Gynecomastia & male chest contouring
  • • Facial plastic surgery & profile balancing
  • • Hair transplant outcomes & scar minimisation
